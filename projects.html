Modelling & Embedding Design Intent in CAD

Design Engineering

Final Year Project focusing on parametric modelling and structural logic. Developed a fully parametric aircraft wing box in SolidWorks, embedding robust design intent to allow for rapid geometric modifications without failure.

Project Detail

Objective

Complex CAD models often fail during updates due to poor reference management and lack of design intent.

Approach

Created a master modeling strategy for a wing box, utilizing equation-driven geometry and logical feature trees.

Outcome

Delivered a robust parametric model capable of automatic resizing while maintaining structural integrity logic.

BMFA Weight Challenge Simulation

Flight Dynamics

Flight simulation and testing for the BMFA Weight Challenge 2024–2025 using X‑Plane. Conducted rigorous performance analysis including stability checks, takeoff runs, and payload handling.

Project Detail

Objective

Predicting aircraft performance under varying payloads (0 kg, 2 kg, 4 kg) prior to physical flight tests.

Approach

Modeled the aircraft in Plane Maker and Airfoil Maker, simulating flight characteristics and gathering telemetry data.

Outcome

Validated takeoff distances and stability margins, ensuring the design met competition requirements for all weight classes.

Avionics Systems Project – AATIS

Avionics

Developed an Attitude Altitude Temperature Information System (AATIS). Integrated Arduino hardware with a custom MATLAB GUI to display real-time flight data.

Project Detail

Objective

Visualizing sensor data in real-time for pilot situational awareness.

Approach

Programmed an Arduino to read sensor inputs and transmit serial data to a MATLAB-based dashboard.

Outcome

Achieved low‑latency display of pitch, roll, altitude, and temperature data with a user-friendly interface.
